Bhooswarga – Heaven on Earth

A multi-faceted urban sustainability & learning initiative.

About Bhooswarga

Bhooswarga (meaning Heaven on Earth) is an interdisciplinary urban sustainability initiative rooted in the belief that healthy soil, healthy food, and healthy communities are deeply connected.

Started as a campus pilot at Nitte Meenakshi Institute of Technology (NMIT), Bengaluru, Bhooswarga has grown into a vibrant living laboratory where students, faculty, and volunteers come together to practice sustainable farming, climate-resilient design, and community-centric innovation.

What began as a garden is now evolving into a city-scale model for urban farming, education, and climate action.

What We Do

Sustainable Urban Farming

  • Organic cultivation using climate-resilient practices
  • Multiple crop cycles including vegetables, greens, fruits, and herbs
  • Drip irrigation for efficient water use
  • Composting of organic waste
  • Natural pest management using neem-based solutions

Living Lab for Learning & Research

  • Hands-on learning for Civil, Sustainable, and Environmental disciplines
  • Live projects on soil improvement, water management, irrigation, and waste-to-resource models
  • Platform for interdisciplinary R&D, innovation pilots, and student-led experimentation

Community & Student Engagement

  • Daily student volunteering, including weekends and semester breaks
  • Internships, live projects, and project-based learning
  • Development of life skills: teamwork, responsibility, leadership, and problem-solving

Well-Being & Inclusion

  • Calm green spaces supporting mental well-being
  • Nature-based learning and reflection zones
