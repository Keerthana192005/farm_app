Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh are your vegetables?

All our vegetables are harvested on the same day they are delivered to you, ensuring maximum freshness and nutritional value.

Do you deliver to my area?

We currently deliver within 50km radius of our farm. Please check your pincode during checkout or contact us to confirm delivery availability.

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver from 7 AM to 7 PM. Orders placed before 12 PM are delivered on the same day, while orders after 12 PM are delivered next day.

Can I return vegetables if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We offer 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality, we'll provide a full refund or replacement.
