# Campus Krishi - User & Admin Guide

## 🏠 Customer Guide

### Getting Started
1. Open the Campus Krishi website
2. Browse today's fresh vegetables
3. Click "Track Order" to check previous orders

---

### 🔍 Finding Products

#### **Search Products**
1. On the home page, find the search bar
2. Type the vegetable name (e.g., "tomato")
3. Results filter in real-time
4. Click "Add to Cart" to purchase

#### **Sort Options**
- **Name (A-Z)**: Alphabetical order
- **Price (Low to High)**: Cheapest first
- **Price (High to Low)**: Most expensive first
- **Stock Availability**: Items with most stock first

---

### 📦 Tracking Your Order

#### **How to Track**
1. Click **"Track Order"** in navigation menu
2. Enter your **Order ID** (from confirmation email)
3. Enter your **Phone Number** (10 digits)
4. Click **"Track Order"** button

#### **What You'll See**
- ✅ Order placed status
- 📦 Item being prepared status
- 🚚 Out for delivery status
- 🏠 Delivered status
- 📋 Complete order details
- 💰 Total amount and payment method
- 📍 Delivery address and phone number

**Payment Method Distribution** (Pie Chart)

**Top Selling Products**
- Lists best-selling vegetables

### 📦 Inventory Management

#### **Location**: Admin Panel → Inventory

#### **Stock Categories**

**Green: In Stock** (>20 kg)
- Adequate inventory for sales
- No immediate action needed
- Monitor regularly

**Yellow: Low Stock** (1-20 kg)
- ⚠️ Should be restocked soon
- Click **"Restock"** to add more
- Customers can still order

**Red: Out of Stock** (0 kg)
- 🛑 Critical - Needs immediate restocking
- Click **"Restock Now"** to add inventory
- Product unavailable for purchase

#### **How to Restock**
1. Find the item in Low Stock or Out of Stock section
2. Click the **"Edit"** or **"Restock"** button
3. Update the stock quantity
4. Click **"Save"** or **"Update"**
5. Verify stock level changes

### ➕ Product Management

#### **Add New Product**
1. Go to **Products** → **Add Product**
2. Fill in details:
   - Product name (e.g., "Tomatoes")
   - Price per kg
   - Stock quantity in kg
   - Description
   - Product image (optional)
3. Click **"Add Product"**

#### **Edit Existing Product**
1. Go to **Products**
2. Find the product in the list
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update any field:
   - Price
   - Stock level
   - Description
   - Image
5. Click **"Update"**

#### **Delete Product**
1. Go to **Products**
2. Find the product
3. Click **"Delete"** (be careful!)
4. Product is removed from store
